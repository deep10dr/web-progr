Seasons

The four main seasons are spring, summer, autumn, and winter. However, some regions have different numbers of seasons, or may even have half-seasons. For example, in India, the four seasons are:

Seasons Description
Winter December to February, with cooler temperatures
SummerMarch to May, with hot temperatures
Rainy June to September, with refreshing showers
AutumnOctober and November, with comfortable temperatures and clear skies

Seasons are caused by Earth's tilt on its axis relative to the orbital plane. This tilt means that the seasons in the Northern Hemisphere are opposite to those in the Southern Hemisphere.
